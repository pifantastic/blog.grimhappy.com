TIL: Pitchfork's Top Tracks of 2011 Playlist, The Willhelm Scream

07 February 2012

Pitchfork's Top Tracks of 2011 Playlist

The only value Facebook has provided me in the last 4 years is its Spotify integration. I have a lot of friends with great taste in music. Being able to see what they are listening to throughout the day has made music discovery a daily event for me.

Today I happened upon Pitchfork's Top 100 Tracks of 2011 playlist. I've never been able to become a fan of Pitchfork itself, but this playlist is an awesome tour through 2011.

Several of my favorite albums of 2011 made the cut, including:

  • M83's Hurry Up, We're Dreaming
  • Bon Iver's Bon Iver, Bon Iver
  • James Blake's James Blake
  • Tune Yards' W H O K I L L
  • Azealia Banks' 212
  • The Joy Formidable's The Big Roar

The Willhelm Scream
